About Us

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is India's premier national organization for promoting excellence in technical education. As a non-profit society, ISTE empowers students and educators through innovation, collaboration, and skill development.

Our Vision

  • Inspire high ambitions and disciplined growth.
  • Foster positive attitudes and holistic excellence.
  • Empower students to reach new academic and professional heights.
  • Deliver value-driven, practical technical education.
  • Create a supportive and enriching learning environment.

Our Objective

To enhance student potential through skill-based learning, industry interaction, and quality education—preparing them to lead and innovate in a competitive world.
